Mau5ville: Level 3

Mau5ville:

Level 3 (stylized as mau5ville: Level 3) is an extended play (EP) and compilation album by Canadian electronic music producer Deadmau5.[1][2] It was released on February 1, 2019 through Mau5trap.[3] It is a followup to his previous EP Mau5ville: Level 2 that was released the previous year. The EP features collaborations with Shotty Horroh and Scene of Action.
