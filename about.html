Who is the man behind the mask?

Background:

Joel Thomas Zimmerman was born on January 5, 1981, in Toronto, Canada. He had a knack for electronics and dissecting hardware during his childhood, and eventually started working in animation and web design. He entered the music world as a DJ, making his way to Europe and South America for gigs with limited resources.

Major Works

His single ‘Move for Me’ reached No. 1 on Billboard magazine's Dance/Mix Show Airplay chart. The singles ‘I Remember’ and ‘Ghosts 'n' Stuff’, also reached No. 1 on Billboard's Dance/Mix Show Airplay chart. His albums ‘For Lack of a Better Name’ and ‘4×4=12’ were critically acclaimed.

Personal Life & Legacy

Deadmau5 got engaged to tattoo artist Kat Von D in December 2012, but they called it quits in June 2013. In March 2014, the Walt Disney Company requested the US Patent and Trademark Office to investigate deadmau5’s application to register mau5head emblem as a trademark, as it resembles the figure of Mickey Mouse. Disney stated that the logo creates confusion as it is "nearly identical in appearance, connotation, and overall commercial impression." In 2014, deadmau5 purchased a $5 million worth house in Campbellville area of Milton, Ontario. He is a video game enthusiast, and whenever he wants to take a break from work, he plays video games to relax his mind.

2019

2019 has already been a huge year for Deadmau5, in January 2019 he introduce "The Cube 3.0" The 16-city tour kicks off September 12 in Dallas and will see the producer’s brand new cube v3 production in other major markets including Los Angeles, Chicago, New York, and more.cube v3 is undoubtedly the most complex production ever presented by deadmau5. The new cube is able to switch positions from 90 degrees into 45 degrees and rotate 360 degrees. As always, it features mind-bending visual content from deadmau5 and his custom touch designer system.
Tickets go on sale to the general public beginning Friday, April 5 at 10am local time via Live Nation.
